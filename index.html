Intro

Hi, I'm Joe Gallotta - a tech and startup enthusiast with a Full Stack background and a non-stop drive to learn.

Early on, I tapped into a niche market that allowed me to develop partnerships with major retail industry leaders. This became the launch and sale of my first startup - an online exchange for buyers and sellers to connect.

While in business, I found myself immersed in personally managing all web design and software optimization - I quickly developed a passion for web development and technology.

Soon after the sale of my company, I graduated from a full stack web development program lead by Actualize Coding Bootcamp in Chicago and currently I'm a computer programming student at Grand Canyon University.
