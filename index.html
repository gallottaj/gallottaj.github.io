Intro

I’m a tech and startup enthusiast with a Full Stack background and a non-stop drive to learn.

Early on, I tapped into a niche market that allowed me to develop partnerships with major retail industry leaders. This became the opportunity to launch my first startup - an online exchange for buyers and sellers to connect. The business gained rapid customer retention and a strong small business customer base. I quickly became a supplier to businesses nationwide.

Along the way I found myself immersed in developing our website and working closely with developers to enhance our online platform and logistics software.I quickly found that web development was not only fascinating but I really enjoyed it.

Just under three years after launch, I successfully sold my first company.Soon after I enrolled in an apprenticeship program for full stack web development, working heavily with Ruby, Rails, JavaScript, VueJS, Bootstrap, HTML/CSS, Postgres, SQL, APIs, OOP, Git, Python and Heroku.

I’ve also completed multiple engineering development programs specialized in CAD, 3D computer modeling, 3D printing and commercial structural drawings.

Expirience

Early on, I tapped into a niche market that allowed me to develop partnerships with major retail industry leaders. This became the opportunity to launch my first startup - an online exchange for buyers and sellers to connect.

My company specialized in the liquidation of high volume customer returns, made available to the public through a unique online auction process.

I established partner relationships with three major industry leaders, with deep roots in the second-hand market. These partnerships allowed me to bring in large quantities of merchandise on consighnment, that had been purchased from major world-wide retailers including Amazon, Home Depot, Wal-Mart, Wayfair, Groupon and Kohls, to name a few.

Along the way I established many relationships, including one with a Canadian motorsport company that brought to me over $100k worth of tools, agriculture equipment and vehicles that I sold on consignment.

I obtained a customer base of 3,000 users nationwide including high community involvement and was recognized as a high volume shipper.

I designed and created our website and optimized our SEO

I hired and managed a team of eight employees.

Ultimately, I successfully sold the company just under three years after launch.

Projects

An app I built personally, Shoot For The Stars allows a user to set, manage and achieve their goals. The app allows the user to break down the "big picture" into smaller, more attainable pieces.

A user can create a profile, network with other users, share their accomplishments and load data from their daily tracking devices.

The app was built in Rails and Vue.js frameworks, with some communication to third-party APIs.
